## Summary

Requirement to ensure data used in systems meets defined quality standards for accuracy, completeness, and consistency.

## What Counts

- Data validation rules at ingestion
- Automated quality checks in pipelines
- Regular data audits and profiling
- Documented quality metrics and thresholds

## What Does Not Count

- One-time manual spot checks
- Quality standards without enforcement mechanisms
- Aspirational quality goals without measurement
